Welcome to Minty Crisp!

Technology is how we hareness actual magic and I aim to make some cool spells ^_^

The power of Web XR is amazing! I love using A-Frame to make neat things. My diverse background in support, technology and the arts helps me bring these big design ideas all together to create some interesting XR interactive experiences. All of my power is thanks to HTML, CSS, Javascript, Web XR, ThreeJS, A-Frame, Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator and the contributions of everyone in this great open-source / sharing community.
